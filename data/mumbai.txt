MUMBAI TRAVEL GUIDE

OVERVIEW:
Mumbai is the capital of Maharashtra and the financial capital of India. It's a vibrant coastal city with Bollywood glamour, colonial architecture, beaches, and incredible food scene.

TOP ATTRACTIONS:

1. Gateway of India - Iconic monument overlooking the Arabian Sea, best visited at sunset
2. Taj Mahal Palace Hotel - Stunning heritage hotel with beautiful architecture
3. Marine Drive - Scenic 3km promenade with sea views, known as the "Queen's Necklace" at night
4. Haji Ali - Historic mosque on an island in the Arabian Sea, accessible during low tide
5. Chhatrapati Shivaji Terminus - UNESCO World Heritage station with Gothic-Victorian architecture
6. Colaba Causeway - Iconic street with shops, restaurants, and street food
7. Bandra-Worli Sea Link - Modern engineering marvel with spectacular views
8. Juhu Beach - Popular beach for swimming and street food

RESTAURANTS & FOOD:
- Vada Pav - Iconic Mumbai fast food
- Pav Bhaji - Spiced vegetable curry with buttered bread
- Bombay High - Seafood restaurant with sea views
- Mahesh Lunch Home - Famous for fresh seafood
- Britannia & Co - Parsi cuisine in a heritage building
- Leopold Cafe - Legendary cafe in Colaba
- Theobroma - Popular chocolate cafe
- Street food: Bhel puri, pani puri, sev puri

SHOPPING:
- Bandra - Trendy shopping destination with cafes
- Linking Road - Budget shopping and street fashion
- Palladium - Luxury shopping mall
- South Mumbai - High-end boutiques
- Dadar Market - Local market for clothes and accessories
- Colaba Causeway - Street shopping and antiques

BEACHES:
- Chowpatty Beach - Popular for evening strolls and snacks
- Juhu Beach - Swimming and water sports
- Worli Beach - Scenic sunset views
- Aksa Beach - Quieter beach in the northwest

November to February (cool weather, 20-30Â°C)

TRANSPORT:
- Local Trains (Locals) - Fastest and cheapest transport
- Metro Rail - Expanding network in different areas
- Autos/Cabs - Ola and Uber available
- Buses - BEST buses cover the city
- Ferries - From Gateway to Elephanta Caves

NEARBY ATTRACTIONS:
- Elephanta Caves - 1 hour ferry from Gateway, UNESCO heritage site
- Lonavala - 100km, hill station with caves and viewpoints
- Khandala - 100km, scenic hill station
- Alibaug - 65km, beach destination with fort

BUDGET TIPS:
- Street food is extremely cheap (Rs 20-40)
- Budget hotels in Colaba/Bandra Rs 1000-2000/night
- Local train is the cheapest transport (Rs 10-15)
