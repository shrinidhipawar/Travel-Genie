Louvre Museum: One of the world's largest museums, home to the Mona Lisa and many masterpieces. Located in the 1st arrondissement. Best to visit in the morning to avoid lines. Nearby cafes include Cafe Marly.

Montmartre & Sacré-Cœur: Historic district with artsy streets, views of Paris from the Sacré-Cœur basilica. Good for afternoon walks. Try local bistros on Rue Lepic.

Le Marais: Trendy neighborhood with boutiques, museums (e.g., Musée Picasso), and many kosher and falafel restaurants like L'As du Fallafel.

Musée d'Orsay: Impressionist art museum located on the Seine. Open late on Thursdays. Great for art lovers.

Saint-Germain-des-Prés: Elegant area with cafes like Café de Flore. Good for evening strolls and dinner.
