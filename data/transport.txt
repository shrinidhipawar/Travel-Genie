Paris:
  - Metro is fastest; most zones covered by a single ticket.
  - Walkable city; many attractions near each other.
  - Uber and Bolt widely available.

Rome:
  - Metro limited; buses more useful for major attractions.
  - Most historic areas are walkable.
  - Taxis available at official stands.

London:
  - Use Oyster or contactless card.
  - Tube is fastest way to travel.
  - Buses run 24/7; night buses helpful after Tube closes.
