HYDERABAD TRAVEL GUIDE

OVERVIEW:
Hyderabad is the capital of Telangana state, known as the "City of Pearls". It's a modern tech hub with rich historical monuments, delicious food, and excellent shopping destinations.

TOP ATTRACTIONS:
1. Charminar - Historic monument built in 1591, iconic structure offering panoramic views from the top
2. Mecca Masjid - One of the largest mosques in India, adjacent to Charminar
3. Golconda Fort - 16th-century fortress with impressive architecture and light and sound shows
4. Hussain Sagar Lake - Man-made lake with Buddha statue, perfect for evening walks
5. Ramakrishna Mutt Temple - Spiritual center with beautiful architecture
6. Falaknuma Palace - Stunning palace with Victorian and Mughal architecture, now a heritage hotel

BEST FOOD & RESTAURANTS:
Hyderabad is famous for its biryani. Must-try dishes:
- Hyderabadi Biryani - Fragrant rice dish cooked with meat, available at Paradise, Shadab, Shah Ghouse
- Haleem - Slow-cooked meat and wheat dish (seasonal in Ramadan)
- Nihari - Spiced meat stew served with naan
- Kebabs - Seekh kebabs and shammi kebabs
- Khichdi - Rice and lentil dish

RECOMMENDED RESTAURANTS:
- Paradise Biryani - Famous chain serving authentic biryani
- Shadab Restaurant - Multi-cuisine in Old City with great ambiance
- Shah Ghouse Biryani - Traditional restaurant near Charminar
- Ohri's - Multi-cuisine in Banjara Hills
- Absolute Barbecues - Premium multi-cuisine
- Pista House - Desserts and sweets, famous for Imarti and Khubani ka Meetha

SHOPPING DESTINATIONS:
1. Laad Bazaar (Charminaar Bazaar) - Old bazaar selling bangles, jewelry, and traditional items
2. Abids - Commercial area with shops and stores
3. Hyderabad Central Mall - Modern shopping mall with international brands
4. Inorbit Mall - Shopping complex with retail and food outlets
5. Begumpet Shopping - Local markets for traditional items
6. Sarojini Market - Budget shopping for clothes and accessories

SHOPPING TIPS:
- Best time for shopping: October to March (pleasant weather)
- Laad Bazaar opens around 10 AM, crowded in evenings
- Negotiate prices in traditional bazaars
- Mall timings: 10 AM - 9 PM usually
- Don't miss traditional items like bangles and jewelry

GETTING AROUND:
- Metro Rail - Modern metro covering main areas, affordable
- Autos/Cabs - Cheap and convenient, use Uber or Ola
- Buses - TSRTC buses connecting all areas
- Taxis - Available at hotels and tourist areas

ACCOMMODATION:
- Budget: Paying Guest homes, budget hotels near Charminar (₹500-1000/night)
- Mid-range: Hotels in Banjara Hills, Somajiguda (₹1500-3000/night)
- Premium: Falaknuma Palace, ITC Koruthi, Taj Hotels (₹5000+/night)

BEST TIME TO VISIT:
October to March - Pleasant weather, ideal for sightseeing
Avoid: May-June (extreme heat), July-September (heavy monsoon)

SAFETY TIPS:
- Old City (Charminar area) can be crowded, keep valuables secure
- Avoid walking alone late at night
- Use official taxis or app-based cabs
- Stay hydrated during hot months
- Keep copies of important documents

LOCAL EXPERIENCES:
- Visit during Ramadan for special food experiences
- Attend light and sound shows at Golconda Fort
- Take a boat ride on Hussain Sagar Lake
- Visit Pearl markets for local crafts
- Experience local culture in street food markets

3-DAY ITINERARY SAMPLE:
Day 1: Arrive → Explore Old City (Charminar, Mecca Masjid) → Shopping at Laad Bazaar → Dinner at Paradise
Day 2: Golconda Fort in morning → Hussain Sagar Lake in afternoon → Shopping at Hyderabad Central Mall → Evening at Ramakrishna Temple
Day 3: Falaknuma Palace tour → Begumpet shopping → Local market exploration → Farewell dinner

NEARBY DAY TRIPS:
- Osman Sagar Lake - 30 km away
- Nagarjuna Sagar Dam - 150 km away
- Tirupati Temple - 150 km away
