Paris:
  - Canal Saint-Martin: Scenic canals with cafes.
  - Rue Crémieux: Colorful Instagram-famous street.
  - Musée de l'Orangerie: Monet’s Water Lilies.

Rome:
  - Aventine Keyhole: Unique view of St. Peter’s Basilica.
  - Coppedè District: Fairy-tale architecture.
  - Basilica di San Clemente: 3-level underground history.

London:
  - Neal’s Yard: Colorful courtyard in Covent Garden.
  - Sky Garden: Free viewpoint (booking needed).
  - Leadenhall Market: Harry Potter filming location.
