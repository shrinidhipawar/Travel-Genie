BANGALORE TRAVEL GUIDE

OVERVIEW:
Bangalore is the capital of Karnataka, known as the "Silicon Valley of India". It's a modern tech hub with pleasant weather, great food scene, and nearby natural attractions.

TOP ATTRACTIONS:

1. Vidhana Soudha - Impressive neo-Gothic legislative building with stunning architecture and surrounding gardens
2. Lalbagh Botanical Garden - Beautiful 240-acre garden with over 1000 plant species, glass house with seasonal flowers
3. Cubbon Park - 300-acre urban park with trees, trails, museums, and peaceful walking paths
4. Bangalore Fort - Historic 16th-century fort with stone walls and interesting architecture
5. ISKCON Temple - Spiritual center with beautiful architecture and vegetarian dining
6. Tipu Sultan's Palace - Historic palace with ornate wooden pillars and Indo-Islamic architecture
7. Nandi Hills - Scenic hill station 60km away, perfect for trekking and sunrise views

RESTAURANTS & FOOD:
- Vidyarthi Bhavan - Famous for dosas and South Indian breakfast
- MTR (Mavalli Tiffin Rooms) - Traditional South Indian cuisine since 1924
- Koshy's - Iconic multi-cuisine restaurant with old-world charm
- Karavali - Coastal cuisine featuring Mangalore and Kerala specialties
- The Ramakrishna - Budget-friendly South Indian food
- Bengaluru Cafe - Traditional Bangalore food
- Street food: Chaat, vada pav, and filter coffee

SHOPPING:
- UB City - Luxury shopping mall with high-end brands
- Phoenix Market City - Modern mall with retail and dining
- Commercial Street - Budget shopping for clothes and accessories
- Brigade Road - Premium shopping street with cafes
- Garuda Mall - Popular shopping destination
- Local markets: K.R. Market for spices and fresh produce

TRANSPORT:
- Metro Rail (BMRC) - Efficient and affordable public transport
- Autos/Cabs - Ola and Uber available
- Buses - BMTC buses cover the city
- Taxis - Available at hotels and tourist areas

BUDGET TIPS:
- Street food is cheap and delicious (Rs 30-50 per meal)
- Budget hotels available for Rs 800-1500/night
- Many parks and gardens free or minimal entry fee
- Use public transport to save money

NEARBY DAY TRIPS:
- Nandi Hills - 60km, sunrise trekking
- Channapatna - 60km, toy making town
- Mysore - 150km, palace and gardens
- Ooty - 280km, hill station with gardens
