GOA TRAVEL GUIDE

OVERVIEW:
Goa is India's premier beach destination on the Arabian Sea coast. Known for beautiful beaches, Portuguese architecture, churches, and vibrant nightlife.

TOP ATTRACTIONS:

BEACHES:
1. Baga Beach - Popular beach with water sports and beach shacks
2. Calangute Beach - Long sandy beach with restaurants and shops
3. Anjuna Beach - Scenic beach with flea market on Wednesdays
4. Vagator Beach - Picturesque beach with red cliffs and water sports
5. Palolem Beach - Peaceful beach in South Goa with coconut palms
6. Cola Beach - Lesser-known scenic beach with waterfall
7. Agonda Beach - Quiet beach with green turtles

HISTORIC SITES:
1. Basilica of Bom Jesus - UNESCO heritage church with architecture
2. Se Cathedral - Largest church in Asia with Gothic architecture
3. Fort Aguada - 17th-century Portuguese fort with sea views
4. Chapora Fort - Historic fort with panoramic views

RESTAURANTS & FOOD:
- Fresh Seafood - Fish curry, prawns, crabs cooked in coconut and spices
- Vindaloo - Spicy meat curry (Goan specialty)
- Bebinca - Traditional Goan dessert with 7 layers
- Feni - Local alcohol made from cashew
- Pork Vindaloo - Must-try Goan pork dish
- Fish Thali - Complete meal with fish and sides
- Beachside shacks - Grilled fish and seafood
- Fusion restaurants in North Goa

WATER SPORTS:
- Parasailing - Thrilling experience at major beaches
- Jet Skiing - Available at Baga and Calangute
- Scuba Diving - Explore underwater life
- Banana Boat Rides - Fun water activity
- Kayaking - Explore backwaters

SHOPPING:
- Anjuna Flea Market - Every Wednesday, antiques and souvenirs
- Arambol Beach - Daily market for clothes and handicrafts
- Mapusa Market - Traditional market for spices and local items
- Panaji - Colonial architecture and shops
- Boutiques in North Goa - Designer clothes and jewelry

NIGHTLIFE:
- Baga and Calangute - Popular nightclubs and bars
- Beach parties - Regular on full moon nights
- Casino boats - Entertainment on the Mandovi River
- Live music venues - Blues, rock, and Indian music

BEST TIME TO VISIT:
November to March (pleasant weather, 22-32Â°C)
Avoid: May-June (hot), July-September (monsoon with heavy rains)

TRANSPORT:
- Autos - Cheap transport within Goa
- Taxis - Ola and Uber available
- Scooter/Bike Rental - Popular for exploring
- Buses - Government and private buses
- Ferry - To cross backwaters

NEARBY ATTRACTIONS:
- Dudhsagar Falls - 60km, stunning waterfall
- Spice Plantations - Cardamom, pepper, and cinnamon estates
- Backwater Cruises - Through coconut palms and mangroves
- Jungle Trekking - Western Ghats adventure

BUDGET TIPS:
- Beach shacks offer cheap food (Rs 100-300)
- Budget hotels Rs 800-1500/night
- Free beach access - no entry fees
- Scooter rental Rs 300-500/day
