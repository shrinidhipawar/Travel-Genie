ROME TRAVEL GUIDE

OVERVIEW:
Rome is the capital of Italy, known as the "Eternal City". Rich history, ancient ruins, and incredible cuisine.

TOP ATTRACTIONS:

1. Colosseum - Ancient amphitheater, iconic symbol of Rome
2. Vatican City - St. Peter's Basilica, Sistine Chapel
3. Roman Forum - Ancient ruins in the city center
4. Pantheon - Beautifully preserved ancient temple
5. Trevi Fountain - Stunning Baroque fountain (arrive early)

- Aventine Keyhole - Unique view of St. Peter's Basilica through a keyhole

RESTAURANTS & FOOD:
- Da Enzo al 29 - Traditional Roman trattoria in Trastevere
- Roscioli - Known for carbonara and cured meats
- Pizzeria La Montecarlo - Popular spot near Piazza Navona
- Cacio e Pepe - Try authentic Roman pasta dishes
- Carbonara - Must-try Roman pasta dish

BEST TIME TO VISIT:
April-May and October-November (pleasant weather, fewer crowds)

TRANSPORT:
Rome Metro and buses available. City is walkable.

TIPS:
- Book major attractions in advance
- Wear comfortable shoes for walking
- Try gelato at multiple places
- Visit churches early morning for fewer tourists
- Carry water bottle in summer
