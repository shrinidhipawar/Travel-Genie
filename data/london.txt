LONDON TRAVEL GUIDE

OVERVIEW:
London is the capital of the United Kingdom, a vibrant city mixing history, culture, modern attractions, and diverse cuisine.

TOP ATTRACTIONS:

1. Big Ben & Houses of Parliament - Iconic Gothic architecture
2. Tower of London - Historic fortress and museum
3. Tower Bridge - Famous bascule bridge with stunning views
4. Buckingham Palace - Official residence of the British Monarch
5. British Museum - World-renowned museum with artifacts from around the globe
6. Westminster Abbey - Historic Gothic church
7. Piccadilly Circus - Famous public square

HIDDEN GEMS:
- Neal's Yard - Colorful courtyard in Covent Garden
- Sky Garden - Free viewpoint (booking needed)
- Leadenhall Market - Harry Potter filming location
- Southbank - Riverside cultural area with Thames views

BEST TIME TO VISIT:
May-September (warm weather, long daylight hours)

TRANSPORT:
London Underground (Tube) is the fastest. Buses, trains, and cabs available.

TIPS:
- Get Oyster card for cheaper public transport
- Many museums have free entry
- Weather can be rainy, bring umbrella
- Book attractions in advance during peak season
- Try afternoon tea experience
